# Provenance Registries Charter (HOR / AIPR / TLR)

## 1. Purpose
These registries provide neutral, non-commercial reference tables for:
- human-origin content declaration (HOR),
- AI provenance attribution (AIPR),
- interoperable trust labels (TLR).

## 2. Scope
This work defines identifiers, schemas, and labels.
It does not certify, verify, enforce, police, or adjudicate disputes.

## 3. Normative Principle
A registry gains authority when it becomes citable and stable.
All identifiers and schemas are designed to remain persistent over time.

## 4. Registries
- Human Origin Registry (HOR)
- AI Provenance Registry (AIPR)
- Trust Label Registry (TLR)

## 5. Citation Form
Third parties may cite:
"According to the Human Origin Registry (HOR)..."
"As defined by the AI Provenance Registry (AIPR)..."
"Using labels defined by the Trust Label Registry (TLR)..."
